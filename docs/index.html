Welcome to the Diploma in Data Science programme at Thomas Adewumi University, Kwara, Nigeria. This comprehensive curriculum is designed to equip final-year students with essential 21st-century skills in data science, machine learning, business analytics, and digital entrepreneurship. The programme emphasises hands-on learning and the practical application of modern tools to enhance productivity, foster creativity, and prepare you for the dynamic demands of the data-driven job market. Below is an introduction to each of the modules offered in this programme:

Data Analysis using Excel and Google Sheets

Unlock the Power of Spreadsheets

Excel remains a foundational tool in data analysis, valued for its versatility and robust capabilities. This course explores Excel’s advanced features to help you efficiently manage, analyse and visualise data.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Mastery of Excel formulas and functions for complex calculations

  • Proficiency in creating and managing pivot tables and pivot charts

  • Skills in data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing

  • Techniques for automating data cleaning tasks using Power Query.

  • Ability to design dynamic dashboards for insightful data visualisation

Data Analysis using R

Harness the Potential of Statistical Computing

R is a powerful language for statistical analysis and data visualisation. This course provides a practical introduction to R, enabling you to conduct sophisticated analyses and produce compelling visualisations.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Understanding of R programming fundamentals and syntax

  • Expertise in data manipulation using packages like dplyr and tidyr

  • Skills in statistical modelling and hypothesis testing

  • Ability to create advanced visualisations with ggplot2

  • Knowledge of reproducible research practices using Quarto

Data Science and Machine Learning with Python

Build Intelligent Systems

Python is the leading language for machine learning and artificial intelligence. This course introduces you to core machine learning concepts and guides you through building predictive models and intelligent applications.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Strong foundation in Python programming for data science

  • Understanding of key machine learning algorithms and their use cases

  • Skills in data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model evaluation

  • Experience with popular libraries such as pandas, seaborn, and scikit-learn.

  • Ability to deploy machine learning models in practical scenarios

Power BI

Transform Data into Actionable Insights

Power BI is a powerful business analytics tool that enables you to visualise data and share insights effectively. This course teaches you how to build interactive dashboards and reports that support evidence-based decision making

What You’ll Gain:

  • Proficiency in connecting and importing data from various sources

  • Skills in data modelling and managing relationships

  • Ability to design and customise interactive visualisations

  • Techniques for collaborating and sharing reports on the Power BI service

  • Understanding of Power BI’s integration and deployment features

Generative AI and Prompt Engineering

Unlocking AI Power: Essential Tools for Productivity and Creativity

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries by boosting productivity, enhancing workflows and enabling rapid innovation. This module introduces key AI platforms, including ChatGPT, NotebookLM, Gemini and Gamma, with a focus on real-world applications.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Hands-on experience with leading AI tools such as ChatGPT, NotebookLM, Gemini and Gamma

  • Ability to automate tasks and streamline complex workflows

  • Skills to use AI for content creation, design and creativity

  • Understanding of AI applications in finance, healthcare and business operations

  • Practical knowledge through projects and case studies

  • Awareness of responsible and ethical AI use

LinkedIn Optimisation

Enhance Your Professional Presence

LinkedIn is an essential platform for networking and career development. This module helps you optimise your LinkedIn profile to attract opportunities and build a strong professional presence.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Strategies for creating a compelling LinkedIn profile

  • Techniques for highlighting your skills, experiences and achievements

  • Insights into building and engaging with a professional network

  • Tips for effective job searching and career growth

  • Best practices for content sharing and personal branding

Digital Marketing and Business Venture

Launch and Grow Your Digital Enterprise

Effective marketing is crucial for business success in today’s digital age. This course equips you with essential marketing skills to build, promote and manage digital ventures effectively.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Understanding of digital marketing principles and strategies

  • Skills in search engine optimisation (SEO) and search engine marketing (SEM)

  • Proficiency in social media marketing and content creation

  • Knowledge of email marketing, analytics, and performance tracking

  • Insights into launching and managing digital business ventures

Google Workspace Productivity

Boost Your Efficiency with Smart Digital Tools

Google Workspace is a suite of cloud-based tools designed to enhance communication, collaboration, and productivity. This module introduces essential Workspace applications and shows you how to use them effectively for academic, professional, and entrepreneurial tasks.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Understanding of Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Meet for communication and collaboration

  • Skills in creating and managing documents, spreadsheets, presentations, forms, and cloud storage using Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, Forms, and Drive

  • Ability to organise ideas, tasks, and workflows efficiently with Google Keep and Google Tasks

  • Practical knowledge to work smarter, collaborate effectively, and manage digital workflows with confidence
